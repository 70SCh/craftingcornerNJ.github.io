The Final Cost

The cost is a $150 entry fee for 2 hours supplemented by $25 dollars an hour. A significant fraction of this cost will be spent on upgrades to improve the service for everyone. This allows everyone to build their dreams without breaking th bank as the more our company grows, the cheaper the cost will become.

For extra equipment, the cost varies based on what equipment you want.

Note- The 3D printers and laser cutters will be allowed for use when you pay the base fee.
